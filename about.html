About Internadda

We are dedicated to bridging the gap between education and industry by providing students with the skills and experience they need to succeed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a comprehensive ecosystem for students to learn, gain practical experience, and launch their careers. We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to apply their knowledge in the real world and get a head start in their professional journey.

Our Story

Internadda was founded by a team of passionate educators and industry professionals who noticed a recurring problem: graduates were leaving universities with theoretical knowledge but lacked the practical skills that companies were looking for. We decided to create a platform that would not only offer high-quality, industry-relevant courses but also connect students with meaningful internship opportunities.

What We Do

We provide a "Learn, Intern, and Earn" model. Students can enroll in our free courses to build foundational skills, prove their knowledge through our standardized tests, and secure paid internships with our network of partner companies. Our goal is to make every student job-ready.
